THE NIGHT POTATO

You run the loneliest roasted-potato kiosk in the city. Night shift, strange customers, and something in the fryer that shouldn't be moving.

A customer-service simulator in 3D low poly, with a kind of horror that doesn't rely on cheap jump scares: the threat is in the details that don't add up.

GenreSimulation · Horror
PlatformPC (itch.io, coming soon)

What you'll find

A night shift that feels longer and longer.

  • Customer-service simulation: orders, change, and patience
  • Minimalist 3D low poly aesthetic
  • Ambient horror: the threat is in the details, not the jump scares
  • Set during a single night shift, at a single kiosk
