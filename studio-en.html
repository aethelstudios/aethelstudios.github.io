AETHEL STUDIOS
The studio

Small ideas, honest decisions.

Aethel Studios is a one-person indie studio. We're not chasing realistic graphics or huge worlds: we care about minimalist pixel art and low poly, and simulation or logic games that make you stop and think before you press a button.

Simulation before reflexes

We prefer systems that react and challenges that get solved by thinking, not matches won through fast reaction time.

Pixel art and low poly, unapologetically

We choose simple aesthetics on purpose. Fewer hours polishing textures, more hours polishing mechanics and levels.

One dev, no noise

Aethel Studios is run by one person. Every design decision goes through a single head, for better and for worse.

Two games, one idea

Not everything has to be scary to make you think.

False Exit and The Night Potato look like opposites: one is a quiet pixel art puzzle, the other a low poly horror simulator. But they share the same foundation: simple systems, no filler, where what matters happens in your head, not your reflexes.

Aethel Studios — One dev. Games that make you think.
